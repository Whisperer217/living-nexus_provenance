# Card Design Concept Analysis

## From the concept images (1000020808.jpg + 1000020795.jpg)

### Card Shape & Proportions
- Portrait orientation — roughly 2:3 aspect ratio (golden ratio territory ~1:1.618)
- Rounded corners — approximately 12-16px radius
- Slight 3D tilt/perspective on the hero row (middle card elevated, side cards angled)
- Cards have a subtle dark border/stroke — not harsh, more like a 1px inner glow

### Artwork Treatment
- Full-bleed artwork fills the entire card face — NO whitespace around the image
- The art IS the card. Text is overlaid, not below.
- Dark gradient scrim at the bottom 40% — smooth, not abrupt
- No visible play button on the card face at rest — it appears on hover/focus

### Text Overlay (bottom of card)
- Work title: white, ~14-15px, medium weight, 1-2 lines max
- Creator handle: muted/secondary, ~11-12px, ~50-60% opacity
- Both left-aligned over the gradient scrim

### WID Badge
- Small circular badge — top-right corner
- Appears to use the Steam-style icon (the platform logo mark) as a small badge
- Gold/cream color, ~24px diameter
- Subtle glow behind it

### Play Button
- NOT visible at rest on card face
- On hover: centered circular button appears with a soft glow
- Glow color: warm amber/gold — NOT harsh yellow, more like rgba(255, 200, 80, 0.3) outer ring

### Card Sizing (from concept)
- Mobile grid: 2 columns — cards ~160-170px wide
- Desktop horizontal scroll: cards ~180-200px wide, portrait
- Featured/hero row: center card ~220px, side cards slightly smaller (perspective effect)

### Section Layout
- Section title: left-aligned, ~18-20px, white, medium weight
- "See All" / "Creators Showcase" button: right-aligned, small pill button
- Section spacing: ~32-40px between sections (generous breathing room)
- Background: very dark purple-black (#0d0814 or similar)

### Overall Aesthetic
- Dark, dramatic, premium — like a gothic Steam store
- Sacred geometry elements in the background (golden ratio spirals, crosses, arches)
- The cards feel like collectible tarot/oracle cards
- Warm gold accents against deep purple-black background
- No harsh whites — everything is slightly warm or tinted
