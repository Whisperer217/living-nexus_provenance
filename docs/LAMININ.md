## The Four Structural Arms of the Platform

Laminin has four structural arms. Living Nexus has four structural arms. This is not decorative architecture. This is doctrine embedded in design.

### Arm 1 — The Registry

The cryptographic provenance layer. The WID system. Every work witnessed on this platform receives an immutable, timestamped, ECDSA-signed identity record that cannot be altered, deleted, or severed from its originator.

This arm holds the creator's claim to their work.

**Doctrine:** A work without a witness is a cell without laminin. It exists but it is not held. The Registry is the molecular bond between creator and creation.

**Technical expression:** WID-MUS, WID-LYR, WID-MAN, WID-CMX, WID-PRO, WID-OUT, WID-SES, WID-ALB. Every identifier in the system is an instance of this arm functioning.

### Arm 2 — The Community

The social and relational layer. Guilds, Witness Networks, Founding Creators, the Jukebox, Sanctuary rooms, Signals. The people who gather around the registry and give it meaning.

This arm holds the registry's credibility.

**Doctrine:** A molecular bond in an empty body is meaningless. The WID means something because the community treats it as meaningful. The community is not a feature of the platform. The community is what makes the registry real. Laminin holds cells together. The cells must exist. The community is the cells.

**Technical expression:** Creator profiles, Witness relationships, Guilds, Listen Together rooms, Signals feed, the gift economy. Every social interaction on this platform is an instance of this arm functioning.

### Arm 3 — The Commerce Layer

The economic layer. Stripe Connect, creator splits, fan gifts, sync licensing (forthcoming), the subscription and membership architecture (forthcoming). The mechanism by which the registry produces economic value for creators.

This arm holds the platform's sustainability.

**Doctrine:** A body that cannot sustain itself cannot hold together. The WID must be economically meaningful, not just cryptographically meaningful. If the registry does not generate income for the creators who populate it, the community will leave and the registry will be empty. The commerce layer is the metabolic function of the organism. Without it the body does not live.

**Technical expression:** Stripe Connect, 90/10 creator split, fan gifts, gift-to-download, forthcoming sync licensing marketplace. Every transaction on this platform is an instance of this arm functioning.

### Arm 4 — The Doctrine Layer

The philosophical and theological foundation. The manifesto, the Lexicon, Field Notes, the BDDT framework, the Laminin document itself. The articulation of why this platform exists and what it stands for.

This arm holds the platform's identity.

**Doctrine:** A body without consciousness does not know what it is. The platform without doctrine drifts. Features accumulate without direction. The registry becomes a badge system. The community becomes a streaming app. The commerce layer becomes extraction. The Doctrine Layer is the nervous system that keeps all other arms oriented toward the same purpose.

**Technical expression:** The manifesto, the Lexicon, the WID specification, this document, BDDT publications, FigShare prior art. Every word on this platform that explains what it is and why it exists is an instance of this arm functioning.

## What This Platform Is Not

This must be stated as clearly as what it is, because drift happens through confusion about identity.

**Living Nexus is not a streaming platform.** The player exists. The streams happen. But the product is the registry. A streaming platform that loses its streaming capability has lost its product. Living Nexus without streaming still has its product. The WID is the product.

**Living Nexus is not a social network.** The community exists. The relationships matter. But the platform is not built to maximize engagement or time-on-platform. It is built to maximize the integrity of the registry. A social network optimizes for attention. Living Nexus optimizes for provenance.

**Living Nexus is not an AI platform.** The Prompt Origin Protocol will sit on top of this platform. The Prompt Studio exists as a creative tool. But Living Nexus does not generate AI content. It witnesses human creative origin. The AI tools exist in service of human creators, not as the platform's identity.

**Living Nexus is not a neutral platform.** It is explicitly Christian-first in its founding doctrine. It honors God and His creation. It does not impose this doctrine on creators of other backgrounds or beliefs — all are welcome in the registry — but the platform's identity, its founding doctrine, and its architectural metaphors are rooted in the conviction that human creativity is sacred because humans are made in the image of a Creator God. This is not a marketing position. It is a doctrinal reality that shapes every design decision.

## Instructions for Every Developer and Contributor

If you are reading this document because you are building on or contributing to Living Nexus, these are your standing orders:

1. **Read this document before writing a line of code.**  
   Not the README. Not the API docs. This document first. Know what you are building before you build it.

2. **Every feature must pass the Laminin test.**  
   Ask: does this hold something together or does it separate something? If it separates a creator from their work, from their community, from their economic value, or from the platform's identity — it fails the test. Redesign it.

3. **The WID is sacred architecture.**  
   You may not simplify, shortcut, mock, stub, or deprecate any component of the WID generation and storage pipeline for convenience. If the WID pipeline is slow, make it faster. If it is complex, document it. You may not remove it.

4. **The chain must never break.**  
   Every database migration, every API change, every schema update must be evaluated for its effect on the cryptographic chain between a creator and their existing WID records. A migration that orphans a WID record is not a technical debt item. It is a doctrinal failure.

5. **The Facebook pixel must never return.**  
   No third-party advertising or tracking pixel, no analytics service that sells data, no surveillance infrastructure of any kind may be added to this platform. The platform does not surveil its creators. It witnesses them. These are opposite things.

6. **The platform is Christian-first but creator-inclusive.**  
   Do not add features that make non-Christian creators feel excluded from the registry. Do not remove features that reflect the platform's Christian doctrinal identity. Hold both. Laminin holds the body together — it does not sort the cells by belief.

7. **When in doubt, return to the registry.**  
   Every ambiguous product decision resolves the same way. Ask: which option better serves the integrity of the provenance registry? Choose that option.
